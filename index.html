About Pig Farming

Pig farming is the practice of growing and breeding domestic pigs, usually for the purpose of producing meat.
Pigs are normally kept in cages or farms, and their growth and health are sustained by a balanced diet of grains, proteins, and other nutrients.

About Fish

Agribusiness is quite lucrative, for those that engage in fish farming, they can attest to the fact that fish farming is highly rewarding. Fish is widely consumed in the world, it is a staple food in various homes, as well as cultures. The demand makes fish farming highly lucrative. Moreover, it is quite nutritious, it is a good source of protein.

Snail

Heliciculture (snail farming) is breeding edible land snails for human or industrial consumption. People eat both snail meat and eggs (white caviar).
